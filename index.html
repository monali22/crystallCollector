There will be four crystals given on the screen. By clickig the crystals you will add specific amount of points to your score.

You will win the game by matching the total score to the random number and lose it if total score goes above the random number

The value of each crystall is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts the game changes the value of the crystalls.
